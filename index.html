Our Story

A Passion for Gastronomy

Founded in 2010, Prima Celeste was born from a passion for creating extraordinary dining experiences. Our Executive Chef Michel Laurent brings over 20 years of culinary expertise from around the world.

We pride ourselves on sourcing the finest local and seasonal ingredients, transforming them into memorable dishes that celebrate both tradition and innovation.

At Prima Celeste, we believe dining is not just about food, but about creating moments and memories to cherish. Our attentive staff, elegant ambiance, and exceptional cuisine combine to offer you a truly remarkable gastronomic journey.
